# 🌾 NutriLink India - National Food Rescue Network

## 📌 Overview

NutriLink India is a real-time food rescue platform that connects food donors (restaurants, hotels, caterers) with NGOs across all 28 Indian states. The platform enables live tracking of food donations, instant claim management, and impact visualization through interactive maps and dashboards.

### 🎯 Mission
Reduce food waste and fight hunger by creating a seamless, transparent network for surplus food redistribution across India.

---

## ✨ Features

### For Donors
- 📝 Post surplus food donations with details (type, quantity, location, expiry)
- 🖼️ Upload food images
- 🌍 Reach NGOs across all Indian states
- 📊 Track impact of donations

### For NGOs
- 🔍 Browse available food donations in real-time
- ✅ Claim donations instantly
- 📋 View claim history and delivery status
- 📍 Track rescue routes on live map

### Platform Features
- 🗺️ **Live Interactive Map** - Visualize donor and NGO locations with route tracking
- 📈 **Impact Dashboard** - Real-time statistics (kg rescued, CO₂ saved, meals served)
- 📊 **Analytics Charts** - Weekly trends and food category distribution
- 🔄 **Auto-Refresh** - Live updates every 4 seconds
- 🏢 **28 States Coverage** - Major cities across India supported
